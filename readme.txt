Portfolio and Custom Posts

== Description ==

Introducing our Portfolio and Custom Posts plugin, a dynamic tool designed to empower WordPress users to showcase their content in a visually stunning and highly customizable manner. Whether you're a freelancer, creative professional, or business owner, this plugin offers unparalleled flexibility in crafting your digital portfolio or presenting custom post types.

With an intuitive interface and robust features, our plugin enables you to effortlessly create and manage diverse portfolios tailored to your unique needs. Showcase your projects, products, case studies, testimonials, or any custom content with ease, elevating your online presence and captivating your audience.

== Features included ==

- Flexible Portfolio Creation: Build unlimited portfolios or custom post types effortlessly, organizing your content according to your preferences.
- Responsive Design: Ensure a seamless user experience across all devices with responsive and mobile-friendly portfolio layouts.
- User-Friendly Interface: Enjoy a user-friendly and intuitive interface that makes portfolio creation and management a breeze, even for users with limited technical expertise.

== Installation ==

1. Go to `Plugins` in the Admin menu
2. Click on the button `Add new`
3. Search for `Portfolio and Custom Posts` and click 'Install Now' or click on the `upload` link to upload `portfolio-and-custom-posts.zip`
4. Click on `Activate plugin`
